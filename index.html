Welcome to Prague

Your best guide

Hello everyone. My name is Denys. I am a decent, calm, kind, polite and punctual man, always ready to gain new knowledge and willing to apply it for discovery of new horizonts. I like to travel + do landscape, architectural and portrait photography. Last year I was lucky enough to live in Prague for 4 months and I fell in love with this incredible city. It was my dream, and it was completely justified!) Prague has a huge number of sculptures, paintings, installations and other works of art on the streets. This city is an ideal place for leisurely walkers. You will see stunning examples of ancient and exquisite architecture, walk through the narrow ancient streets and discover for yourself a small piece of magic of this city. And I will be there to capture this moment for your memories
